Computational Modeling of Human and Machine Intelligence

Call for participants

The Computational Modeling of Human and Machine Intelligence (Mozer Lab) at the University of Colorado Boulder (CU Boulder) is looking for online participants for a brief experiment on how people reason about objects.

In this experiment you will be shown a series of letters and asked to identify each letter. You will complete multiple trials, which typically takes $expTimeMin minutes.

The requirements are:

  • You are at least 18 years old,
  • You have normal vision or wear corrective lenses, and
  • You are a fluent English speaker.

Please Note: Refreshing or leaving the page while playing will prevent you from completing the HIT. The experiment will only work in modern browsers, and may have difficulty loading on slower connections.

This task can only be completed once.

If you meet the requirements, please click the "Accept HIT" button on the Amazon site above to begin the task.
